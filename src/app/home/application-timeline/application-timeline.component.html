Application Timeline

Step Date
Letter of Intent Submission Portal Opens

If your organization wishes to apply for funding, you must begin by completing the Letter of Intent.
January 15
Letter of Intent Submission Deadline

No requests will be considered after this date.
April 1
Second round notifications distributed.

After a first round of deliberations, we will request full proposals from those organizations who best meet our objectives and criteria. A notification will be sent via email to the primary contact named on the letter of intent.

Due to the large volume of requests we receive each year, we DO NOT send out notifications of rejection.
May 1
Full Proposal Submission Deadline

Full Proposals must be submitted online by this date. Late submissions will not be considered.
June 15
Notification of grants to be funded August 15
Distribution of Awards October 15
